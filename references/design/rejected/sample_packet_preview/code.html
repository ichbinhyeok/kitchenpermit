Deficiencies & Findings

INS-2023-1104-A
CRITICAL

Filtration Unit A Clogging

ID: FND-001

Primary particulate filters in Unit A are operating at 15% efficiency. Severe buildup detected on electrostatic plates, indicating failure of pre-filter stage. Risk of back-pressure damage to main blower.

Required Action

Immediate replacement of pre-filters and chemical bath cleaning of electrostatic plates. Inspect pressure differential sensors for recalibration.

WARNING

Ducting Vibration (Section 3)

ID: FND-002

Excessive vibration noted in primary ducting line, specifically at junction points near the roof exit. Mounting brackets show signs of metal fatigue.

Recommended Action

Schedule reinforcement of mounting brackets within next 30 days. Consider installing dampening collars at junction points.

NOTE

Access Panel Seal Degradation

ID: FND-003

Rubber gaskets on secondary access panels are beginning to show signs of drying/cracking. No leakage detected currently, but preventative maintenance suggested.
